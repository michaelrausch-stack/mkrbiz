Background

Twenty years leading. Then the choice to serve.

For over twenty years, I held senior management positions — business unit director, head of controlling, marketing director, commercial director — at international corporations including Avaya and Bosch, across multiple countries. I know what it feels like to carry decisions that no one else sees, to manage teams through ambiguity, and to perform under pressure that doesn't switch off.

Then I made a deliberate shift. I began consulting and mentoring SMEs and start-ups — and discovered that my real vocation wasn't telling leaders what to do, but helping them find what they already knew. I trained in Co-Active coaching to the highest standards in the profession — those of the International Coach Federation (ICF), the only framework endorsed by Harvard Business School — and I'm now an ICF member and part of the Mentorday accelerator network.

Since then, I've guided more than 150 coaching journeys — from corporate executives at multinationals to first-time founders, and senior professionals navigating a pivotal moment in their career.

I work in English, French, German, and Spanish. In person across Europe — or online, wherever the work takes you.

— Maikel

How it works

A clear framework, from the very first conversation. You know exactly what you're committing to — and what you're not.

Frequently asked questions

Before you get in touch.

What's the difference between coaching and therapy?

Coaching works on the present and the future: decisions, leadership presence, sustainable performance. Therapy works on the past and emotional suffering. If your needs fall into the latter, I'll refer you to a qualified professional — that's a core ethical principle of the ICF.

How many sessions should I expect?

A typical engagement runs 8 to 12 sessions, spaced two weeks apart. The 90-day programme is more intensive — 12 weekly sessions over three months. The duration is agreed together during scoping; nothing is imposed.

How does confidentiality work?

Rigorously and absolutely. What is said in session does not leave the session. If your organisation sponsors the engagement, a three-party framework is set from the start — shared objectives, strictly confidential content.

In person or online?

Both formats deliver excellent results. In-person sessions are available in Paris, Brussels, and Geneva depending on the period. Online sessions allow me to work with leaders across Europe and beyond — in English, French, German, or Spanish.

What is Co-Active coaching?

An approach recognised by the International Coach Federation that considers the whole person — not just objectives, but also values, balance, and meaning. It's a rigorous method, particularly suited to leaders who want lasting results, not cosmetic fixes.

What are the fees?

Fees are presented during the discovery session, based on the format and number of sessions chosen. The discovery session itself is without commitment and free of charge.
