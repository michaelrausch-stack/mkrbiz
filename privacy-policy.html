7. Cookies and Tracking

7.1 What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small file placed on your device (computer, tablet, mobile) when you visit a site. It allows the site to recognise your browser and remember certain information.

7.2 Cookies used on this site

This site uses no audience-measurement, profiling, or advertising cookies. No statistical analysis tool (such as Google Analytics) is installed.

However, the site loads typography from Google Fonts via Google's servers. This connection may technically share your IP address with Google. This functionality is strictly necessary for the visual display of the site.

7.3 Consent management

In accordance with the ePrivacy Directive and applicable national implementations, a consent banner appears on your first visit. You may modify your choices at any time by clearing your browser's cookies or contacting us.

To block cookies at the source, you may configure your browser. Procedures vary by browser — consult your browser's help (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, etc.).
